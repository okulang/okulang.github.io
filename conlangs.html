Iskki Leke'sou

A language for my own use. To push my brain, I've decided to use OSV word order and some screwy ergativity. On the other hand, to give my brain a sense of familiarity (nostalgia?) I'm trying to use Jatin and Japanese to inspire morphology. On the gripping hand, I've made sure to include things I think are cool, like ejectives and telefixes.

Myrkolð

A language inspired by some songs on Old Norse, so mainly built for sound. Words will have polyconsonantal roots, something like Arabic, where the choice of vowels will be fossilized harmony.
